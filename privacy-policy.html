ZenStow Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

ZenStow is operated by Patrick Wong. Contact: zenstow.business@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information ZenStow collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the ZenStow mobile app.

2. Information We Collect

ZenStow stores inventory-related content you enter, such as room names, container names, item names, item quantities, item prices, and app preferences.

ZenStow currently does not offer in-app purchases, subscriptions, or paid features.

ZenStow uses Firebase Analytics to collect basic app usage events (for example, feature interactions and screen-level usage) to improve the app. This analytics setup includes event metadata and a pseudonymous install identifier generated by the app.

Analytics event metadata may include feature interaction details and user-entered labels (for example, room, container, item, or search result names) when those interactions are tracked.

ZenStow currently does not require account creation and does not collect account credentials.

If you choose to send in-app feedback, your feedback message is sent to our Firebase backend (Cloud Firestore) together with optional contact email (if provided), a pseudonymous install identifier, app environment, app version, platform, and timestamps.

3. How Information Is Used

  • Provide core app features for home inventory organization
  • Save your data and settings on your device
  • Maintain and improve app functionality
  • Review and respond to in-app feedback

4. Data Storage and Retention

ZenStow is currently local-first. Data is stored on your device and remains there until you delete it in-app or uninstall the app.

ZenStow does not currently provide cloud sync or a backend account system.

In-app feedback submissions are stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore to help us review feedback and improve the app.

If cloud sync or server-side processing is introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before that change is released.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

ZenStow does not sell personal information and does not share personal information for targeted advertising.

Information may be processed by platform providers as required for app distribution, including Apple and Google.

Analytics data may also be processed by Google Firebase as a service provider for app analytics.

In-app feedback data is processed and stored using Google Firebase Cloud Firestore.

6. Permissions

ZenStow currently does not request sensitive runtime permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

7. Children

ZenStow is not directed to children under 13.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to your personal information, including rights to request access, correction, deletion, or information about data practices.

California residents: ZenStow does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To submit a privacy request, contact zenstow.business@gmail.com.

9. Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of data associated with ZenStow feedback submissions or analytics identifiers, use this link: Request data deletion.

Please include any details that help identify your request, such as your install identifier (if available) and approximate submission date.

10. International Users

ZenStow is operated from the United States (California). If you use the app from another region, your information may be processed in accordance with this policy and applicable law.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date at the top will be revised when updates are made.
